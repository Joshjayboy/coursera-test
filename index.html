Chicken

Chicken is a dish of food that has been cooked very well and is usually mixed with other ingredients such as pepper, onion, tomatoes, salt and lot more. it can be taken alone or as an additive to another dish.

Beef

Beef is the culinary name for meat from cattle, particularly skeletal muscle. Beef is a source of high-quality protein and nutrients and can be used to make other food items. it can also be taken alone or together with another dish

Fried Rice

Fried rice is a dish of cooked rice that has been stirred and fried in a frying pan and it is usually mixed with other ingredients such as tomatoes, vegetables, carrot, and meat. It is often eaten alone or as an accompaniment to another dish.
